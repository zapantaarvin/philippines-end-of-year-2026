The Loop — Plan B

Starts and ends the same way, but Palawan gets room to breathe: Puerto Princesa's underground river and Honda Bay join El Nido for a full five-day run, timed so a 5am van still leaves the whole afternoon free. Bohol and Siquijor absorb the two days Cebu would've used. Tap a stop above to jump straight there. Trade-off: Christmas Day (Dec 25) lands on a Balicasag dive day, not a family day at home.
