The Loop — Plan C

The simplest loop yet: Palawan absorbs everything Bohol, Siquijor, Cebu, and Sagada used to cover, running Puerto Princesa straight into El Nido and out to a two‑day island‑hopping expedition to Coron. Christmas is untouched in Las Piñas, Pulag is a clean two‑day in‑and‑out, and the final week eases home through Manila, Pampanga, and Las Piñas. Tap a stop above to jump straight there.
